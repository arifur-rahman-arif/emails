One Parent’s Peace of Mind

A Nurturing Beginning

Jordan's mother was very particular about the choice of pre-school for her only child. She researched different schools and their programmes.

She decided on My First Skool as she felt it had the best all-round curriculum and innovative learning methods.

The teachers are well-trained in looking out for each child's needs. It has been a joy for her to see Jordan adapt, thrive and being well-prepared for primary school at the same time.

How Do We Spark a Child’s Curiosity?

My First Skool helps children make sense of the world around them through outdoor activities and opportunities to experiment.

Through broadening their knowledge and essential skills, we lay the foundation that sparks and sustains their curiosity for science, history and geography.

It's our holistic curriculum and innovative learning methods that make us Singapore's preferred preschool.
